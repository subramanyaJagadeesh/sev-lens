# Incident Severity Policy

## Severity Guide
- **P1**: Broad outage or severe customer impact.
- **P2**: Significant degradation with clear customer impact.
- **P3**: Partial degradation or limited blast radius.
- **P4**: Low-impact issue or internal noise.

## Classification Principle
Classify based on customer impact, service criticality, and error persistence rather than on a single metric alone.
